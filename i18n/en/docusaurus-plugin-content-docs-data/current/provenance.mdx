# Provenance and Reliability

## Where the data comes from

Every row carries a `source_id` — a key into the
[source registry](https://github.com/gasyoun/AfanasiyNikitin/blob/main/data/sources.csv),
which records the rights for each source. Four principal sources:

- **"The Journey Beyond Three Seas"** (15th c.) — public domain; cited by manuscript witness.
- **Khrustalev, D. G., 2026** — a source of *facts* (datings, localizations);
  every borrowed fact is tagged `KHR2026`. The book's own exposition and
  composition are not reproduced (see
  [RIGHTS.md](https://github.com/gasyoun/AfanasiyNikitin/blob/main/RIGHTS.md)).
- **Ibn Majid, c. 1490** — sea distances and method; public domain.
- **The project's own analytical layer** (coordinate anchoring, trade
  heuristics, modern retellings of passages) — CC-BY-4.0.

## Reconciliation

Places and people are cross-checked against external authorities by
[`tools/reconcile.py`](https://github.com/gasyoun/AfanasiyNikitin/blob/main/tools/reconcile.py):
places are verified by coordinate distance and type (settlement), people by
the instance-of=human property and a description keyword match. Every
decision — and every row left for manual review — is recorded in
[reconciliation.md](https://github.com/gasyoun/AfanasiyNikitin/blob/main/data/reconciliation.md);
re-running the script is reproducible.

## Computable chronology

[`tools/computus.py`](https://github.com/gasyoun/AfanasiyNikitin/blob/main/tools/computus.py)
computes Orthodox Easters (the Julian paschal reckoning) and Ramadans (a
tabular Islamic calendar) for every year of the journey — and independently
reproduces the reconstruction's anchor datings: Easter on 2 April 1469
(Hormuz), the overlap of Great Lent with Ramadan in early March 1470, and
Easter on 10 April 1474 (Muscat). The atlas's chronology is not a
retelling — it's a re-verified computation.

## Quality

A completeness report — the share of populated fields, page-reference
coverage, reconciliation status, and the epistemic composition of every
table — is regenerated at
[data/QUALITY.md](https://github.com/gasyoun/AfanasiyNikitin/blob/main/data/QUALITY.md).
Known data errors aren't hidden — they're documented (example: a Dabhol
coordinate in an early project file pointed out into open sea — the fix is
recorded in the gazetteer's notes).

## Versions

The dataset and the site are versioned **separately**: the data version
lives in
[datapackage.json](https://github.com/gasyoun/AfanasiyNikitin/blob/main/datapackage.json) /
[CITATION.cff](https://github.com/gasyoun/AfanasiyNikitin/blob/main/CITATION.cff) /
[.zenodo.json](https://github.com/gasyoun/AfanasiyNikitin/blob/main/.zenodo.json)
and changes only when `data/` changes; site releases follow their own cadence
in the [CHANGELOG](https://github.com/gasyoun/AfanasiyNikitin/blob/main/CHANGELOG.md).
